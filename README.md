# AI-Pengfei-Solo-Campus-Brand
AI低碳能源科普内容生成与校园传播风控系统
AI先锋未来人才大赛｜鹏飞集团校招雇主品牌真实命题

一句话简介：结合AI文案生成、SQL校园用户数据分析与合规风控方案，助力鹏飞集团破除传统能源刻板印象，打造年轻化低碳能源校园品牌

## 命题背景
鹏飞集团正在从传统民营能源企业向绿色低碳科技型能源企业转型，布局氢能全产业链、零碳智慧物流、氢能装备制造和5G数智应用等新兴业务，具备很强的青年人才吸引力。
但目前校招传播面临三大现实挑战：
1. **品牌认知偏传统**：多数高校学生仍将鹏飞标签化为传统重化工、煤炭焦化类传统业务，不了解其新能源转型布局与低碳产业价值，距离青年学生认知较远
2. **传播内容不够轻量化**：官方校招宣传素材风格偏正式，形式单一，难以适配B站、小红书、高校社群等年轻学生主流信息渠道，传播效果有限
3. **精准触达缺失**：不同高校、专业、年级学生信息习惯、行业认知差异显著，统一内容无法实现分层精准触达

本次命题核心目标：借助AI工具，面向高校学生打造雇主品牌内容创作与精准触达方案，把氢能、零碳物流、数智化转型业务转化为易懂易传播的科普内容，规划多渠道传播路径，帮助大学生理解鹏飞转型价值与校招成长机会

## 解决方案
本单人参赛项目围绕鹏飞集团年轻化校园传播需求，搭建三大联动模块：

1. **AI低碳能源科普内容生成模块**
    - 定制规范化大模型提示词模板，批量产出适配校园生态的B站短视频脚本、小红书科普笔记、高校社群校招文案、宣讲互动文案
    - 将氢能技术、零碳智慧物流、能源数智化转型等专业知识通俗化、轻量化，重塑企业低碳科技形象，破除传统能源刻板印象

2. **SQL高校学生画像数据分析模块**
    - 基于高校在校生调研问卷数据，使用SQL完成学生专业、年级、信息渠道、能源行业认知度、内容偏好等多维度用户画像统计分析
    - 产出可落地的分层校园传播策略与多渠道传播方案，实现差异化内容精准投放，提升校招信息触达效率

3. **能源校园宣传合规风控模块**
    - 建立能源行业校园推广广告合规模板、学生调研数据隐私保护规范
    - 核查宣传文案真实性、广告合规与数据合规，兼顾传播效果、信息真实性与合规安全

# AI-Pengfei-Solo-Campus-Brand
AI System for Low-Carbon Energy Popularization & Campus Communication Compliance
AI Pioneer Future Talent Competition | Official Pengfei Group Campus Employer Branding Challenge

Brief Introduction: This project combines AI copywriting, SQL campus user data analysis and compliance risk control, to help Pengfei Group break the stereotype of traditional energy enterprises and build a youth-oriented low-carbon energy campus brand.

## Project Background
Pengfei Group is transforming from a traditional private energy enterprise to a green low-carbon technology energy enterprise, focusing on the whole hydrogen energy industry chain, zero-carbon smart logistics, hydrogen equipment manufacturing and 5G digital applications.
However, its campus recruitment communication faces three key challenges:
1. **Fixed Traditional Image**: Most college students only associate Pengfei with traditional coal and coking businesses, lacking awareness of its low-carbon transformation, hydrogen energy industry and digital upgrading layout.
2. **Unfriendly Content Format**: Official formal materials cannot be easily shared and viewed on youth platforms such as Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and university communities.
3. **Diversified Student Information Habits**: Students from different universities, majors and grades have different content preferences and information channels, making unified communication inefficient.

Official Goal: Use AI tools to create youth-friendly employer branding content and targeted communication plans, popularize hydrogen energy and zero-carbon businesses, and help college students recognize Pengfei’s transformation value and career opportunities.

## Solution
This individual project includes three core modules:

1. **AI Low-Carbon Energy Popularization Module**
    - Using LLM prompt templates to generate Bilibili video scripts, Xiaohongshu posts, campus community copies and presentation interactive scripts.
    - Transforming professional hydrogen energy & zero-carbon industry knowledge into easy-to-understand, shareable youth-style content, reshaping the corporate image.

2. **SQL Campus User Portrait Analysis Module**
    - Based on college student surveys, SQL is used to analyze student majors, grades, media habits and industry cognition.
    - Develop multi-channel differentiated campus communication strategies to improve precise delivery efficiency.

3. **Campus Promotion Compliance & Risk Control Module**
    - Establish advertising compliance rules and student data privacy guidelines for campus promotion.
    - Review copy content to ensure authenticity, legality and information security.

## Tech Stack
- SQL: User portrait analysis & data visualization
- LLM Prompt Engineering: Mass production of energy popularization & recruitment content
- Markdown / PPT: Project documentation & presentation
- Jianying: 3-minute project demo video

## Deliverables
 - Youth-friendly hydrogen & low-carbon energy popularization & campus recruitment copy templates
 - SQL analysis scripts & data visualization reports
 - Complete multi-channel campus employer branding plan for Pengfei Group
 - 3-minute project demo video
 - Compliance review templates & risk control documents
