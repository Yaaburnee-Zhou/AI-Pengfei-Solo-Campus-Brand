4. **跨文化合规风险**：年轻化能源科普内容存在合规审核与跨地域传播风险

# AI-Pengfei-Solo-Campus-Brand
AI Low-Carbon Energy Popularization Content Generation & Campus Communication Compliance System
AI Pioneer Future Talent Competition | Pengfei Group Campus Employer Brand Official Challenge

## Project Overview
Create youth-oriented low-carbon energy popularization content via LLM prompt engineering, supported by SQL campus audience portrait analysis and cross-cultural compliance risk control, to reshape Pengfei Group’s employer brand image among university students.

## Enterprise Background
Founded in 1993, Pengfei Group is a large-scale green low-carbon technology enterprise, with total assets of 150 billion RMB and over 30,000 employees. Its business includes coking coal chemical industry, full hydrogen energy industrial chain, hydrogen equipment, zero-carbon smart logistics and 5G digital intelligent technology, undergoing a major transformation to a clean energy enterprise.

## Core Pain Points
1. **Deep-rooted Stereotype**: Most university students view Pengfei only as a traditional heavy chemical enterprise, lacking awareness of its hydrogen energy industry layout and zero-carbon transformation achievements.
2. **Mismatched Content Style**: Official promotional materials are too formal, incompatible with youth platforms including Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and university communities.
3. **Insufficient Targeted Communication**: Students from different majors and grades have different information habits, making precise differentiated communication difficult.
4. **Cross-Cultural Compliance Risks**: Youth energy popularization content faces potential regulatory and cross-regional compliance risks.

## Overall Solution & 3 Core Modules

### 1. LLM AI Content Generation Module
- Optimized prompt templates for Xiaohongshu posts, Bilibili short video scripts and campus recruitment copy
- Mass production of easy-to-read hydrogen & low-carbon energy popularization content for Gen Z
- File Directory: `/prompt`

### 2. SQL Campus User Portrait Analysis Module
- Lightweight SQL scripts to analyze energy cognition differences among different majors and grades
- Visual chart for energy cognition comparison, confirming liberal arts students as key target audience
- File Directory: `/sql`

### 3. Compliance Risk Control & Brand Communication Module
- Cross-cultural communication compliance guidelines and content review templates
- Complete opening report, industry research notes and references
- Systematic campus employer branding strategy
- File Directory: `/docs`

## Tech Stack
- SQL: Campus audience portrait analysis & data visualization
- LLM Prompt Engineering: AI popularization content generation
- Markdown / PPT: Project documentation & roadshow presentation
- Jianying: Demo video (for future roadshow)
