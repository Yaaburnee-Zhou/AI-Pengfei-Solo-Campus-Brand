## 企业基础信息
鹏飞集团创始于1993年，总资产1500亿元，员工3万余名；核心业务包含绿色碳焦化工升级、氢能全产业链、氢能装备制造、零碳智慧物流、5G数智能源技术应用，是大型绿色低碳科技新能源企业。

## 现存核心痛点
1. 刻板印象：高校群体固有认知停留在传统煤化工业务，对氢能全产业链、零碳物流、数智转型等低碳布局认知不足
2. 内容适配问题：硬核产业内容不适合B站、小红书、高校社群年轻化短视频叙事
3. 受众差异：能源化工/经管/计算机等不同专业学生信息需求差异大，需要分层精准传播
4. 迭代效率问题：校园传播素材迭代慢，缺少高效测试优化机制

## 项目目标
1. 品牌重塑：完成从传统能源认知 → 绿色低碳科技新能源企业认知转型
2. AI内容转译：将氢能全产业链、氢能装备、零碳智慧物流、5G数智能源硬核业务转为大学生易懂轻量化内容
3. 分层精准传播：适配不同高校专业学生，打造多版本内容模板，布局B站/小红书/高校社群渠道
4. AI迭代测试：批量生成传播文案并做A/B测试，优化叙事，提升校招雇主吸引力

# AI-Driven Employer Brand Rebuilding Plan for Pengfei Group | AI Pioneer Future Talent Competition

## Basic Enterprise Information
Founded in 1993, Pengfei Group is a large green low-carbon technology new energy enterprise, with total assets of 150 billion RMB and more than 30,000 employees.
Core business sectors:
- Upgraded green coking chemical industry
- Full hydrogen energy industrial chain
- Hydrogen energy equipment manufacturing
- Zero-carbon smart logistics
- 5G digital intelligent energy technology application

## Core Pain Points
1. Stereotype Bias: University students mainly perceive Pengfei as a traditional coal chemical enterprise, with limited awareness of its low-carbon transformation layout including hydrogen energy, zero-carbon logistics and digital intelligence businesses.
2. Content Mismatch: Professional industrial content cannot adapt to the youth-oriented communication style of Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and university campus communities.
3. Diverse Audience Needs: Students from energy, chemical, business, computer and other majors have vastly different information demands, requiring hierarchical and targeted communication strategies.
4. Low Iteration Efficiency: Traditional campus content creation is slow, lacking rapid iteration and A/B testing mechanisms for optimal communication effects.

## Project Objectives
1. Brand Transformation: Shift public perception from a traditional energy enterprise to a modern green low-carbon technology new energy enterprise.
2. AI Content Translation: Translate professional content about hydrogen energy industry chain, hydrogen equipment, zero-carbon logistics and 5G smart energy into easy-to-understand campus versions.
3. Hierarchical Multi-Channel Communication: Develop multi-version content templates for different student groups, and carry out precise communication via Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and university campus communities.
4. AI A/B Testing & Iteration: Generate multiple versions of scripts and copy for A/B testing, continuously optimize narratives, and improve campus recruitment attractiveness.

## Folder Structure
- /prompt: AI prompt templates (short video scripts, campus recruitment copy, hydrogen energy popular science copy)
- /docs: Project proposal, research notes, campus communication compliance guidelines
- /ppt: Final presentation slides

## 3 Core AI Strategies
1. AI Content Adaptation: Use refined prompts to convert complex low-carbon industry stories into youth-friendly short video narratives.
2. Audience Hierarchical Design: Create 3 versions of templates: general popular science version, engineering technical version, and campus recruitment version.
3. AI Iteration & A/B Testing: Rapidly generate different versions of copy, conduct A/B testing, and refine the best communication style.

- Team Form: Individual / 1-3 members
- Theme: AI Campus Employer Brand Communication for Pengfei Group
