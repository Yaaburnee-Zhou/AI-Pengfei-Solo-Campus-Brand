AI低碳能源科普内容生成与校园传播风控系统
AI先锋未来人才大赛｜鹏飞集团校招雇主品牌真实命题

一句话简介：结合AI文案生成、SQL校园用户数据分析与合规风控方案，助力鹏飞集团破除传统能源刻板印象，打造年轻化低碳能源校园品牌

## 项目背景
鹏飞集团正加速转型布局氢能全产业链、零碳智慧物流等绿色低碳业务，致力于打造新型清洁能源企业。
但当前校园校招传播存在三大核心痛点：

1. **刻板印象固化**：多数高校学生仍将鹏飞标签化为传统重化工企业，不了解其新能源转型布局与低碳产业价值
2. **传播内容脱节**：官方校招宣传素材风格偏正式，不适配B站、小红书、高校社群等年轻学生主流信息渠道，传播效果有限
3. **精准投放缺失**：不同专业、年级学生信息习惯、行业认知差异显著，缺乏分层化、差异化的精准校园传播策略

## 解决方案
本单人参赛项目围绕鹏飞集团年轻化校园传播需求，搭建三大联动模块：

1. **AI低碳能源科普内容生成模块**
    - 定制规范化大模型提示词模板，批量产出适配校园生态的B站短视频脚本、小红书科普笔记、高校社群校招文案
    - 将氢能技术、零碳产业等专业知识通俗化、轻量化，重塑企业低碳形象，破除传统能源刻板印象

2. **SQL高校学生画像数据分析模块**
    - 基于高校在校生调研问卷数据，使用SQL完成学生专业、年级、信息渠道、能源行业认知度等多维度用户画像统计分析
    - 产出可落地的分层校园传播策略，实现差异化内容精准投放，提升校招信息触达效率

3. **能源校园宣传合规风控模块**
    - 建立能源行业校园推广广告合规模板、学生调研数据隐私保护规范
    - 核查宣传文案合规性，兼顾传播效果、信息真实性与合规安全

# AI-Pengfei-Solo-Campus-Brand
AI System for Low-Carbon Energy Popularization & Campus Communication Compliance
AI Pioneer Future Talent Competition | Official Pengfei Group Campus Employer Branding Challenge

Brief Introduction: This project combines AI copywriting, SQL campus user data analysis and compliance risk control, to help Pengfei Group break the stereotype of traditional energy enterprises and build a youth-oriented low-carbon energy campus brand.

## Project Background
Pengfei Group is actively transforming into a new clean energy enterprise, focusing on the whole hydrogen energy industry chain and zero-carbon smart logistics.
However, its campus recruitment communication faces three key challenges:

1. **Fixed Stereotype**: Most college students still view Pengfei as a traditional heavy chemical enterprise, unaware of its new energy transformation and low-carbon business layout.
2. **Mismatched Content**: Official promotional materials are too formal, failing to fit youth platforms such as Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and university communities.
3. **Lack of Precise Delivery**: Students from different majors and grades have different information habits, lacking targeted communication strategies.

## Solution
This individual project includes three core modules:

1. **AI Low-Carbon Energy Popularization Module**
    - Using LLM prompt templates to generate Bilibili scripts, Xiaohongshu posts and campus recruitment copies in a youth-friendly style.
    - Transforming professional hydrogen energy & zero-carbon industry knowledge into easy-to-understand content, reshaping the corporate image.

2. **SQL Campus User Portrait Analysis Module**
    - Based on college student surveys, SQL is used to analyze student majors, grades, information channels and energy industry awareness.
    - Develop differentiated campus communication strategies to improve information delivery efficiency.

3. **Campus Promotion Compliance & Risk Control Module**
    - Establish advertising compliance rules and student data privacy guidelines for campus promotion.
    - Review copy content to ensure authenticity, legality and information security.

## Tech Stack
- SQL: User portrait analysis & data visualization
- LLM Prompt Engineering: Mass production of energy popularization content
- Markdown / PPT: Project documentation & presentation
- Jianying: 3-minute project demo video

## Deliverables
- Youth-friendly hydrogen & low-carbon energy copy templates
- SQL analysis scripts & data visualization reports
- Complete campus employer branding plan for Pengfei Group
- 3-minute project demo video
- Compliance review templates & risk control documents
